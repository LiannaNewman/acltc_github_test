Rainbow Pizza

The perfect dish for a rainy day!

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough, store bought or make your own
  • 1 cup Pizza Sauce, store bought or make your own
  • 3 cups Mozzarella Cheese
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • 3 Orange Peppers
  • 2 Yellow Peppers
  • 1 Cup Basil
  • 1 Blue Potato (chopped)
  • 1 Purple Onion (diced)

Directions

  1. Preheat Oven to 350 degrees.
  2. Roll out pizza dough and spread 1 cup of sauce.
  3. Sprinkle with Mozzarella cheese.
  4. Arrange tomatoes, orange peppers, yellow peppers, basil, blue potato, and purple onions in that order in nested circles starting with the tomatoes on the outer circle ending with purple onions on the inner circle.
  5. Bake for 30 minutes.

Let cool 5 minutes. Cut into 8 pieces or more if desired. Serves 8.
